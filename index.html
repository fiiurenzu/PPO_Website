Founded by Florent Martini and Pierre Dufresne, PPOffice is a creative studio that engages in long-term partnerships with emerging and established artists, curators and public / private art centres, both locally and internationally. Acknowledging their responsibility, as designers, in reshaping collective imaginaries & suggesting narratives of sustainable futures, the two partners bring their creative skills to bear on relevant and engaging cultural initiatives facing the social and environmental challenges of their time. PPOffice Studio's distinctive culture, deeply rooted in a personal interest and affiliation with artists' work and the related exhibitions in which it has worked with & for, allows its practice to navigate through all the spaces that shape an art project or work. Having tackled a wide range of case studies since its creation in 2019, future and current partners can benefit from a dynamic workflow and communication, including with any professionals who may be involved in their projects. Depending on each context and purposes, the two partners are able to gather creatives working in various fields to join them in this approach.
